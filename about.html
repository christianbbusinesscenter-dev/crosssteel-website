Our Story

Built on One Principle

"Do it right the first time, or don't do it at all."

CrossSteel Welding & Construction LLC was built on a simple idea — that every job deserves quality work. Not the cheapest weld. Not the fastest patch job. The right weld.

We're a mobile-first welding and fabrication company based in Magnolia, TX, serving clients across the great state of Texas — from Houston to Dallas, the border region, Rockdale, and beyond. From a cracked trailer frame to a custom 16-foot ranch gate, every project gets the same care. We don't cut corners. That's why our clients keep coming back.
